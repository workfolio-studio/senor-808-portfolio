# Señor 808 Artwork And Content Submission Guide

## 1.0 Purpose

This guide explains what Bob Garcia should provide when artwork, biography details, collaboration information, or media proof is ready for the Señor 808 website. Bob does not need to edit GitHub or manage the deployment process. Bry will review the materials, confirm required details, and implement approved updates.

## 2.0 New Artwork Submission

For each artwork, provide:

- Final image file in JPG, PNG, or WebP format
- Approved public title
- Confirmed year, or a clear instruction to omit the year
- Approved category: SPRAY + ACRYLIC, MIXED MEDIA, ACRYLIC, COLLAGE, or COMMISSION
- Optional dimensions and materials, when verified
- Optional short description, when approved for publication
- Public-use confirmation for the image and information
- Any sale, commission, venue, partner, or client information that may be named publicly

A descriptive filename is helpful, such as `rose-circuit-2026.jpg`. The largest available clean image should be supplied; Bry will prepare the web-optimized version.

## 3.0 Existing Artwork Corrections

When correcting an existing portfolio item, identify the visible title or send a screenshot of the artwork card. State exactly what should change, including title, year, category, description, image crop, or permission status.

Do not estimate dates, titles, or credits. A detail that is not confirmed can be omitted until it is known.

## 4.0 Biography And Location Updates

Provide the exact approved language or factual correction for:

- City and state
- Short biography
- Longer biography
- Artistic influences
- Materials and techniques
- Commission availability
- Exhibition, venue, client, or partner references

The current canonical location is San Antonio, Texas. Visual art is the established primary lane. Audio, podcasting, and related media formats remain a developing extension.

## 5.0 Media And Podcast Proof

Media information should be submitted only when it is ready for public use. Provide:

- Approved episode, show, or format title
- Public link or final file
- Approved description
- Publication date, when confirmed
- Participant, producer, venue, or partner permissions
- Thumbnail, cover image, or clip, when available

Unreleased concepts and private development notes will not be presented as completed public proof.

## 6.0 Project And Commission Inquiries

The website inquiry form is currently a prototype and does not yet deliver submissions. Before it becomes operational, Bob and Bry must confirm:

- Recipient email address or selected form service
- Required inquiry fields
- Expected response language and timing
- Spam and privacy handling
- Successful end-to-end delivery testing

## 7.0 Submission Method

Send the approved files and information to Bry through the agreed project channel or shared Google Drive folder. Include one clear note identifying what is new, what is being corrected, and what is approved for public publication.

Bry will maintain the repository, prepare the assets, implement the change, verify the deployment, and return the updated website for Bob’s review.
